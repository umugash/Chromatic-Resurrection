Our Flower Colorizer AI uses a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) combining a Generator and Discriminator to colorize grayscale flower images realistically. The Generator predicts color details using CNN and U-Net architecture, while the Discriminator ensures the output looks natural. We also use L1 Loss for accuracy and LPIPS (Perceptual Loss) to make colors visually appealing. This combination helps the model create vibrant, life-like flower images with high quality and realism
